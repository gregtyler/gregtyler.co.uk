## Work

I am a **Lead Software Developer** at the **Office the Public Guardian**, an arms-length body of the Ministry of Justice. I work across various teams supporting public-facing and back-office systems to support Power of Attorney services.

I see my role as being a catalyst to enable teams to move fast and effectively. That sometimes takes the form of pairing with other devs, working cross-profession to plan upcoming delivery or just doing the work.

## Side projects

I'm trying to archive some of my old side projects to give me more space to focus on new things. Some are being simplified and centralised, some are being handed over, some I'm just shutting off. I'm not going to abandon anything valuable, just the chaff.

I'm actively working on changes to this site, particularly to make it easier for me to post full blog posts and microposts.

I'm planning to start a new project to host my own activity tracking, rather than using a glut of small closed apps. I hope to use [schema.org schemas](https://schema.org/ReadAction) as the backbone.
